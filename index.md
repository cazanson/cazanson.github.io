### Ca’ Zanson?

In the early-1970's my father got our family stationed in Vicenza, Italy, 45 miles from Venice and smack-dab in the middle of Italy's Veneto region. Venetian pronunciation further softens soft g, s, and ci sounds, such that 'Gian' ('Gianni') would be rended 'Zan.'

Now "Ca’ Zan" means "John's house," so "ca’ Zanson" would be "Johnson’s house," right? An alluring domain name. I snapped it up.

Enjoy your stay.
